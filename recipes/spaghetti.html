Spaghetti

Description

The recipe is a dish made out of spaghetti noodles and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • 500ml water

Steps

  1. add in water
  2. let the water boil
  3. add in spaghetti in boiling water
  4. let the spaghetti boil with the boiling water
  5. add in tomato sauce
  6. let the tomato sauce boil with the boiling spaghetti and water
  7. Remember to keep the lid closed when you are not adding ingredients
  8. close on the fire and drain the water
  9. serve on plate
