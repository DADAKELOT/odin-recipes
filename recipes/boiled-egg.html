Boiled eggs

Decsription

Boiled eggs are common but delicious food known by everyone

Ingredients.

  • eggs
  • 350ml of water
  • Sauce pan

Steps

  1. Put 350ml of water inside the saucepan and let it boil
  2. put in your eggs once it bois
  3. after 15 minutes close the fire
  4. drain the water and your boiled eggs are ready!
