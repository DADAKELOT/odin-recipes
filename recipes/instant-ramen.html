Instant ramen

Description

Instant ramen is a famous modern food popular due to it's good taste.

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen bought in a supermarket
  • at least 250ml of water
  • a hotpan

Steps

  1. Put water in hotpan and wait till it boils
  2. Once it boils but your instant ramen and packging in
  3. After 5 minutes close on the fire and drain the water
  4. serve it on a plate and it's ready!
